Overdue Payment Instructions

This document outlines two scenarios of overdue payments: account arrears and arrears for cloud resources bound to EIP.

Account Arrears

The rules for account arrears vary depending on the billing mode of public network fees, as detailed below:

Annual/Monthly Subscription Billing

Billing based on annual or monthly bandwidth subscriptions is unaffected by account arrears and is only related to the expiration time of the resource:

Starting 7 days before the expiration of the annual/monthly subscription bandwidth, the system will send expiration alerts and renewal reminders. If you do not renew the subscription before the EIP's expiration date, the EIP will be released.

Pay-as-You-Go Traffic or Hourly Bandwidth Billing

For unbound or bound EIP in arrears for more than 1 hour the service will be suspended, and billing will stop.

Unbound EIP will be released.

EIP bound to CVM will be unbound and released.

Arrears for Bound Cloud Resources

If the cloud resource bound to an EIP incurs arrears, the EIP will automatically unbind and switch to idle billing status. If you no longer need it, please release the EIP from the console as soon as possible.
