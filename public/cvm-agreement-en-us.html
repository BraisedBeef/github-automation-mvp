The terms defined in the Agreement shall have the same meaning under this SLA, unless otherwise defined herein. Your continued use of the service constitutes your agreement to the SLA applicable at that time.

General Provisions

1. Definitions

Service: For the purposes of this SLA, the services referred to herein shall only refer to the cloud services specified below in this SLA.

Service Period: A service period is one calendar month.

Total Service Period Time: Refers to the total number of days in each service period multiplied by 24 (hours) and 60 (minutes).

Service Unavailability: Service unavailability will be defined for each service in the specific service terms below. Service unavailability does not include instances where the service becomes unavailable due to the limitations described below and in the specific service terms.

Service Unavailability Time: Refers to the total duration of service unavailability within a service period. If the unavailability period is less than one month, it shall still be calculated as one month. Service unavailability time shall not be counted multiple times, meaning that the service unavailability time for each service period shall not be included in the service unavailability time of the subsequent service period.

Service Availability: Refers to the service availability rate committed for the services specified in this SLA during a service period.

Monthly Service Fee: Refers to the total service fee paid by the customer for the services provided during a service period (i.e., a calendar month). If the customer makes a one-time payment for multiple service periods, the monthly service fee will be calculated by prorating the payment across the number of months purchased.

2. Compensation

Compensation Method: If CatCloud fails to meet the service availability commitment under this SLA, you may apply for compensation in accordance with the provisions of this SLA. Compensation will be provided in the form of a voucher, and such compensation shall constitute the sole and exclusive remedy offered by CatCloud for failing to meet the service availability commitment.

Compensation Application Timeframe: You may submit a compensation request for cloud services that did not meet the service availability commitment during a service period, following the settlement of the billing for that service period. The compensation request must be submitted within two (2) months after the end of the service period in which the service failed to meet the service availability commitment. Requests submitted after this timeframe will not be accepted. CatCloud will reasonably evaluate your application and, in accordance with the provisions of this SLA and the principle of good faith, will determine whether compensation is applicable.
